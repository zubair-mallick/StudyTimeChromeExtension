Study Time

Uses your Gemini API key to generate summaries directly in the extension.

Features:

  • 🔒 Video Blocking: Automatically blocks non-educational videos
  • 📝 Video Summary: Click the "Summarize Video" button on any YouTube video
  • 🤖 AI-Powered: Uses Google Gemini for intelligent classification and summaries

Tip: For testing, you can use a smaller model name in background.js. The summary feature requires transcript availability.
